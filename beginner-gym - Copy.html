    Push Day (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

    01 - Bench Press (Dumbbell or Barbell): 3 sets of 8-10 reps


    02 - Overhead Press (Dumbbell or Barbell): 3 sets of 8-10 reps


    03 - Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps


    04 - Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps


    05 - Tricep Dips (Bench Dips): 3 sets of 8-10 reps or until failure


    06 - Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

    Pull Day (Back, Biceps)

    01 - Pull-Ups (Assisted if needed): 3 sets of 6-8 reps or until failure


    02 - Bent-Over Rows (Dumbbell or Barbell): 3 sets of 8-10 reps


    03 - Seated Cable Rows: 3 sets of 10-12 reps


    04 - Face Pulls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps


    05 - Bicep Curls (Dumbbell or Barbell): 3 sets of 10-12 reps


    06 - Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

    Legs Day (Quads, Hamstrings, Calves)

    01 - Squats (Bodyweight or Barbell): 3 sets of 8-10 reps


    02 - Leg Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps


    03 - Deadlifts (Dumbbell or Barbell): 3 sets of 8-10 reps


    04 - Lunges (Walking or Stationary): 3 sets of 10 reps per leg


    05 - Calf Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps


    06 - Leg Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
